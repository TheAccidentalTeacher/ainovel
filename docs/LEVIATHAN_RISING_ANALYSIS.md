- Overly cheesy Christian imagery (glowing angels, obvious crosses everywhere)
- Too much green (aurora should be accent, not dominant)
- Cluttered composition (keep it clean and dramatic)
- Weak typography (must work at thumbnail size)
- Generic stock photo look (needs custom, unique feel)
- Too dark (needs warm accent for hope)

### What to EMPHASIZE:
- Alaska's majestic but harsh beauty
- Contrast between technology failure and human resilience
- Faith as subtle undercurrent (light imagery) not overt preaching
- Family/community without being saccharine
- Professional thriller aesthetics
- Unique, memorable composition

This is Book 1 of a planned series. Cover design should:
- Allow for consistent branding across multiple books
